About

Pathfinder is an educational platform aimed at connecting students with learning opportunities and resources to achieve their academic goals.

Our mission is to empower students by providing access to courses, learnerships, and bursaries tailored to their educational needs and aspirations.

Pathfinder is built using modern technologies such as Angular for the frontend and Spring Boot for the backend, ensuring a robust and scalable platform.

For more information, please follow us on our social media channels or contact us via email.

Features Overview

  • Admin Panel: Add Courses, Learnerships, Bursaries
  • Student Panel: View Courses, Learnerships, Bursaries, Capture Results

Technology Stack

Frontend: Angular, Angular Material

Backend: Spring Boot, MongoDB
